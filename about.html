How OsteoScan AI
Predicts Your Risk

Our system uses a clinically validated ensemble of Machine Learning models trained on 10,000+ patient records to deliver accurate, probability-based risk predictions in under 2 minutes.

The 4-Step Process

1

Data Collection

You provide demographic details (age, gender, ethnicity), medical history, and lifestyle factors through our 3-step guided assessment form.

Age & Gender Medical History Lifestyle
2

Preprocessing & Feature Engineering

Input data is normalized, encoded, and enriched — including Age Binning, handling missing values, and one-hot encoding for categorical features.

Age Binning Normalization Encoding
3

AI Model Inference

Your data is passed to the best-performing ML model (XGBoost, SVM, or Neural Network) which uses predict_proba() to return a probability-based risk score.

XGBoost SVM Neural Network
4

Clinical Report Generation

A personalized risk report is generated with percentage score, risk level classification, explanation, and tailored medical recommendations.

Risk Score PDF Report Recommendations

Key Technical Features

balance

SMOTE Class Balancing

Synthetic Minority Oversampling ensures the model doesn't ignore the rare high-risk class.

percent

Probability-Based Output

Using predict_proba() instead of binary 0/1 to give a nuanced, percentage-based risk score.

category

Age Binning

Ages are grouped into clinically meaningful brackets (Young Adult, Adult, Middle-Aged, Senior, Elderly).

lock

Privacy First

All data is processed locally and in-session. No patient data is stored server-side after prediction.
