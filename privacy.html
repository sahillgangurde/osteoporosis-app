Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect clinical markers (Age, Gender, Medical History, Lifestyle habits) provided by you during the risk assessment process. This data is used solely to generate your osteoporosis risk score.

2. Data Security

All calculations are performed using encrypted protocols. We do not sell or share your clinical data with third-party advertisers. Your data is treated as sensitive medical information.

3. AI Processing

The risk score is generated by an AI model (XGBoost) trained on clinical datasets. The processing is local to our secure servers to ensure maximum privacy.

4. User Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your assessment results or ask for your session data to be cleared from the local cache.
